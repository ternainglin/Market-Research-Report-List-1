<p><ul><li>Wet Cut Product</li><li>Dry Cut Product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stand tile saw market is divided into two main types: wet cut products and dry cut products. Wet cut tile saws use water to cool the blade and minimize dust, making them ideal for cutting hard materials like porcelain and granite. These models typically offer cleaner cuts and extended blade life. In contrast, dry cut products operate without water, generating more dust but allowing for greater portability and ease of use, especially in outdoor or non-plumb environments.</p></p>
