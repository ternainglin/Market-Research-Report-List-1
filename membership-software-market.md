<p><p>Membership software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based membership software is hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, allowing for easier updates, scalability, and remote access. This option typically offers lower initial costs and eliminates the need for extensive IT infrastructure. On-premises membership software, on the other hand, is installed locally on company servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring more upfront investment and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
