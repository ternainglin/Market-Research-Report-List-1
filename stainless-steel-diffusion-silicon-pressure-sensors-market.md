<p><p>Stainless steel diffusion silicon pressure sensors are vital components in various industrial applications, categorized primarily into absolute and relative pressure sensors. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing highly accurate readings essential for applications like altitude measurement and gas analysis. In contrast, relative pressure sensors measure pressure against atmospheric pressure, making them ideal for monitoring tanks and pipelines where changes from the ambient pressure are critical. Both types leverage the durability and corrosion resistance of stainless steel for reliable performance in harsh environments.</p></p>
