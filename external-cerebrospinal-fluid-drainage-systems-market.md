<p><strong>The External Cerebrospinal Fluid Drainage Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems</li><li>Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The External Cerebrospinal Fluid Drainage Systems market encompasses two primary types: External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems and Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems. EVD systems are utilized to relieve increased intracranial pressure by draining cerebrospinal fluid from the brain's ventricles. In contrast, LD systems are used to manage conditions affecting the spinal canal, allowing for drainage from the lumbar region. Both systems are vital in neurosurgical procedures and critical care, promoting patient safety and recovery by regulating fluid levels and preventing complications.</p></p>
