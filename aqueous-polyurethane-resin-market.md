<p><strong>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion</li><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resin market is divided into two main types: Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion (APD) and Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion (APE). APD is characterized by its finer particle size and uniform distribution, making it ideal for coatings and adhesives that require high performance and durability. APE, on the other hand, offers improved processability and stability, making it suitable for a wider range of applications, including textiles and foams. Both types emphasize eco-friendliness and low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions.</p></p>
