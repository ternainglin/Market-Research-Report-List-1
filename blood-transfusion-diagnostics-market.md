<p><p>The blood transfusion diagnostics market encompasses various testing technologies essential for ensuring the safety and compatibility of donated blood. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is used for detecting antibodies and antigens. NAT (Nucleic Acid Testing) offers highly sensitive viral detection. Western Blot Assays confirm specific proteins, while Fluorescence Assays utilize fluorescent markers for identification. Other technologies may include microarray andPCR-based methods. Together, these diagnostic tools ensure the effective screening of blood products for transfusion, minimizing the risk of transfusion-transmitted infections.</p></p>
