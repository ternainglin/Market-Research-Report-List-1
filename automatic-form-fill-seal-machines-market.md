<p><p>The Automatic Form Fill Seal Machines market comprises two primary types: Horizontal Form Fill Seal (HFFS) and Vertical Form Fill Seal (VFFS) machines. HFFS machines operate horizontally, allowing for the packaging of products in a flat format, typically suitable for pouches and bags. In contrast, VFFS machines package products vertically, ideal for filling bags from the top and are commonly used for granules, powders, and liquids. Both types enhance efficiency and speed in packaging operations across various industries.</p></p>
