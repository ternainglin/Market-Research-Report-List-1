<p><ul><li>Spray-Dried Foods</li><li>Freeze-Dried Foods</li><li>Vacuum-Dried Foods</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated foods market comprises several types, including spray-dried, freeze-dried, and vacuum-dried foods, among others. Spray-dried foods involve rapidly drying liquid food using hot air, preserving flavors and nutrients. Freeze-dried foods undergo freezing followed by sublimation, retaining taste, color, and texture. Vacuum-dried foods utilize reduced pressure to remove moisture while maintaining quality. Other methods may include sun drying or air drying. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of shelf life, rehydration quality, and nutritional retention.</p></p>
