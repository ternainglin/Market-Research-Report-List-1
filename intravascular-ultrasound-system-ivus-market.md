<p><p>Intravascular Ultrasound System (IVUS) is a sophisticated imaging technique used during coronary interventions to visualize the interior of blood vessels. This technology employs high-frequency sound waves to create detailed cross-sectional images of arterial walls, aiding in the assessment of conditions such as atherosclerosis and plaque buildup. IVUS enhances procedural accuracy and improves patient outcomes by providing real-time feedback to clinicians.</p><p>The IVUS market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases and the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures. Technological advancements, including the development of high-definition imaging and integration with other modalities like optical coherence tomography (OCT), are enhancing the effectiveness of IVUS systems, further propelling market expansion. Additionally, the growing geriatric population and lifestyle factors contributing to heart diseases are expected to fuel the demand for IVUS devices.</p><p>The Intravascular Ultrasound System (IVUS) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period.
