<p><p>Enterprise storage and Internet of Things (IoT) MRAM (Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory) markets primarily feature two types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM). Toggle MRAM leverages magnetic fields to write data, offering fast access and low power consumption, making it suitable for embedded applications. STT-MRAM, on the other hand, utilizes spin-transfer torque to move magnetic domains, providing higher density and better scalability. Both types enhance data retention and performance, critical for the evolving demands of enterprise storage and IoT devices.</p></p>
