<p><ul><li>Heated Fog Generators</li><li>Chilled Fog Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stage fog generators are essential in theatrical and entertainment settings, enhancing atmosphere and visual effects. Heated fog generators utilize high temperatures to vaporize special fluids, producing thick, consistent fog. They are efficient and provide a dense mist ideal for larger venues. In contrast, chilled fog generators cool the fog before release, creating a low-lying effect that appears closer to the ground. This type is often used for dramatic scenes, adding depth and enhancing lighting effects, suitable for both small and large productions.</p></p>
