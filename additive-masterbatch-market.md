<p><strong>The Additive Masterbatch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Masterbatch</li><li>Black Masterbatch</li><li>Color Masterbatch</li><li>Additive Masterbatch</li><li>Filler Masterbatch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The additive masterbatch market comprises several key types, each serving distinct functions in plastic processing. White masterbatch is used for opacity and brightness, while black masterbatch enhances UV protection and color stability. Color masterbatch includes a range of pigments for vivid hues in products. Additive masterbatch incorporates various functional additives to enhance properties like flame retardancy or anti-static features. Lastly, filler masterbatch focuses on cost reduction and improved physical properties by incorporating materials like calcium carbonate or talc.</p></p>
