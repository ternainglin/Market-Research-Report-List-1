<p><strong>The Hemp Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long (bast) Fibers</li><li>Short (core) Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemp fiber market is divided into long (bast) fibers and short (core) fibers. Long fibers, extracted from the outer stalk, are strong, durable, and ideal for textiles, ropes, and composites. Short fibers, from the inner stalk, are softer and used in paper, insulation, and animal bedding. Both types are sustainable, biodegradable, and versatile, catering to industries like construction, automotive, and fashion. The market thrives on eco-friendly demand, with long fibers valued for strength and short fibers for cost-effective bulk applications.</p></p>
