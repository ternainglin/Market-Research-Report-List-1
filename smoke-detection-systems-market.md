<p><p>The smoke detection systems market is primarily categorized into ionization detectors, photoelectric detectors, and other types. Ionization detectors use radioactive material to sense fast-burning fires by detecting changes in ionized air. Photoelectric detectors, on the other hand, employ light sensors to identify slow, smoldering fires by measuring smoke particles obstructing a light beam. Other detectors include combinations of both technologies and specialized systems designed for specific environments, enhancing safety and response efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
