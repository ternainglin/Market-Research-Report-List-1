<p><p>Biological Safety Cabinets (BSCs) are crucial for protecting personnel, products, and the environment from biohazards in laboratory settings. The Class II Type A BSC recirculates air but may vent to the outside, suitable for work with low-risk agents. Class II Type B BSCs are ducted to exhaust air outside, ideal for handling volatile chemicals and higher-risk pathogens. Class III BSCs offer the highest protection with a completely sealed design, suitable for highly hazardous materials, often employing glove access for interaction.</p></p>
