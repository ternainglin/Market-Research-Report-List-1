<p><p>The admissions software market is categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed directly on an institution's servers, allowing for greater control over data and customization, but often requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based software is hosted remotely, providing easier access, scalability, and lower upfront costs, as updates and security are managed by the provider. Institutions choose between these types based on their specific needs, infrastructure, and budget considerations.</p></p>
