<p><p>The Aircraft Interior Fasteners market is divided into two main types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, which include bolts and screws, create secure connections through interlocking threads, providing strength and easy disassembly. Non-threaded fasteners, such as rivets and clips, rely on friction or deformation to hold components together, offering a lightweight solution with high structural integrity. Both types are essential for assembling various aircraft interior components, ensuring durability and safety in aviation environments.</p></p>
