<p><p>The two-component silicone sealant market is segmented into several types based on curing mechanisms. </p><p>1. **Condensation Solidification** involves moisture in the air to cure, suitable for indoor applications. </p><p>2. **UV Curing** uses ultraviolet light for rapid solidification, ideal for quick applications. </p><p>3. **Condensation and UV Dual Curing** combines both methods, offering versatility for various environments. </p><p>4. **Addition Curing** reacts through a chemical process without releasing by-products, ensuring minimal shrinkage and enhanced performance. These types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
