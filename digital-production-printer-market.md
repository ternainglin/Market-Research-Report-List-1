<p><p>The Digital Production Printer market comprises two main types: Inkjet and Electrophotography printers. Inkjet digital production printers utilize liquid ink ejected through tiny nozzles, enabling vibrant color outputs and versatility on various media. They are ideal for short runs and customization. In contrast, Electrophotography printers use a dry toner and an electrostatic process to transfer images onto paper, making them suitable for high-speed, high-volume printing. Both technologies cater to diverse printing needs in commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
