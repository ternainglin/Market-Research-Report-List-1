<p><p>The electric razor market comprises two primary types: foil and rotary. Foil razors feature a thin, flexible foil that covers the blades, offering a close shave and is ideal for sensitive skin. They excel in straight-line shaving. Rotary razors, on the other hand, have circular blades that move in a circular motion, making them suitable for navigating facial contours and longer hair. Users typically choose between these types based on their shaving preferences, skin sensitivity, and hair texture.</p></p>
