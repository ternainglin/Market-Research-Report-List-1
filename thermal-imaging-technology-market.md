<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging technology is categorized into two main types: uncooled and cooled. Uncooled thermal imaging devices operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing materials like microbolometers to capture infrared radiation. They are cost-effective and suitable for various applications, including security and surveillance. In contrast, cooled thermal imaging systems require cooling mechanisms to reduce noise, enhancing sensitivity and image quality. These devices are typically used in specialized applications such as military operations and scientific research, where precision and performance are critical.</p></p>
