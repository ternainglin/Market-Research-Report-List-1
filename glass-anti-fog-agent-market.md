<p><p>The Glass Anti Fog Agent market is primarily segmented into two types: gel type and foam type. Gel type agents provide a viscous coating on glass surfaces, offering long-lasting anti-fogging effects by reducing surface tension. They are ideal for use on glasses and visors. Foam type agents, on the other hand, are delivered as a spray, creating a lightweight, dispersible film that prevents fogging. Both types cater to various applications, including automotive, sports eyewear, and safety goggles, enhancing visibility and safety.</p></p>
