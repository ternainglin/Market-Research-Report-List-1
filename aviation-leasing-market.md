<p><ul><li>Dry Leasing</li><li>Wet Leasing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aviation leasing primarily consists of two market types: dry leasing and wet leasing. Dry leasing involves the lease of an aircraft without crew, maintenance, or insurance, allowing operators to exercise more control over operational aspects and costs. In contrast, wet leasing includes the provision of an aircraft along with crew, maintenance, and insurance, ideal for short-term needs or situations where operators require immediate capacity. Both leasing types cater to various operational requirements in the aviation industry.</p></p>
