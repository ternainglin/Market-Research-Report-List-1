<p><p>The electronic pipetting gun market is primarily categorized into single-pipe and multi-pipe devices. Single-pipe pipettes are designed for precision dispensing of liquids one sample at a time, ideal for tasks requiring accuracy in smaller volumes. Multi-pipe pipettes, on the other hand, allow for simultaneous dispensing across multiple samples, enhancing throughput and efficiency in high-volume laboratory settings. Both types are crucial for various applications in research, clinical, and industrial environments, providing improved speed and repeatability in liquid handling processes.</p></p>
