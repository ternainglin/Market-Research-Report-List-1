<p><p>Stretch wrap machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semiautomatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operators to wrap products by hand, offering cost-effectiveness for low-volume operations. Semiautomatic machines streamline the wrapping process, automatically dispensing film while operators position the load. Automatic machines provide the highest efficiency, fully automating the wrapping process to handle high volumes with minimal labor. Each type serves different operational needs, balancing cost, speed, and productivity to suit various packaging requirements.</p></p>
