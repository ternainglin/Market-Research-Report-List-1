Lever Valves utilize a mechanical lever system to open and close, providing enhanced control and responsiveness to pressure changes. In contrast, No Lever Valves, often featuring direct acting mechanisms, operate automatically without manual intervention, offering simplicity and reliability. Both types are designed to ensure system safety by preventing overpressure situations, making them essential in various industrial applications.</p></p>
