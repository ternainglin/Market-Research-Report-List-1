<p><strong>The Galvanized Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-dip Galvanized Steel</li><li>Electrical Galvanized Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized steel market primarily consists of two types: hot-dip galvanized steel and electro-galvanized steel. Hot-dip galvanized steel undergoes a process where steel is submerged in molten zinc, providing robust corrosion resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor and heavy-duty applications. In contrast, electro-galvanized steel is coated with zinc through electroplating, offering a thinner layer of protection, suitable for applications requiring smooth finishes and moderate corrosion resistance, such as automotive and appliance manufacturing.</p></p>
