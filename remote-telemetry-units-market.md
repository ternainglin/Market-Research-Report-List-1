<p><p>The Remote Telemetry Units (RTU) market is primarily divided into two types: wireless RTUs and wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs utilize wireless communication technologies, enabling real-time data transmission over vast distances without the need for extensive cabling, making them ideal for remote or difficult-to-access locations. Conversely, wired RTUs connect directly through physical cables, offering reliable communication and lower latency in stable environments. Both types are crucial for monitoring and controlling remote assets in various industries, including utilities, oil and gas, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
