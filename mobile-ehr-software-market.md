<p><p>The Mobile EHR (Electronic Health Record) software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHRs offer flexibility and accessibility, allowing healthcare providers to access patient data from anywhere via the internet. This model typically reduces infrastructure costs and enhances scalability. In contrast, on-premise EHR systems are installed locally on physical servers, providing greater control and security over patient data but often requiring significant upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
