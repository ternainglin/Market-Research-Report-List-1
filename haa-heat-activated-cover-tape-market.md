<p><ul><li>Non-conductive</li><li>Static Dissipative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HAA (Heat Activated) Cover Tape market features two main types: non-conductive and static dissipative. Non-conductive cover tape is primarily used for applications where electrical conductivity is not required, providing a reliable barrier to protect components during the soldering process. In contrast, static dissipative cover tape is designed to control static electricity, making it ideal for sensitive electronic components that can be damaged by electrostatic discharge. Both types ensure safety and efficiency in the manufacturing and packaging of electronic devices.</p></p>
