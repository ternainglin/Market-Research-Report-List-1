Hydraulic sheet metal folding machines use fluid power for precise bending, suited for thicker materials. Electric sheet metal folding machines offer energy efficiency and precise control with minimal noise. Pneumatic sheet metal folding machines rely on compressed air for operation, enabling faster cycles and lower energy costs, each catering to specific industrial needs and production capacities.</p></p>
