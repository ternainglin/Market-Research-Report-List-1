<p><p>The architectural paints and coatings market is categorized mainly into three types: water-borne, solvent-borne, and other formulations. Water-borne paints use water as a primary solvent, offering low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them environmentally friendly. Solvent-borne paints rely on organic solvents, providing durability and quick drying times but typically emitting higher VOCs. "Other" includes specialty formulations like powder coatings and high-performance products, catering to specific application needs and performance criteria across residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
