<p><p>The sublimation ink market consists primarily of two types: water-based dye sublimation ink and solvent dye sublimation ink. Water-based dye sublimation ink is environmentally friendly and ideal for use on fabrics and soft goods, producing vibrant colors and good washability. In contrast, solvent dye sublimation ink, which contains petroleum-based solvents, is commonly used for printing on various substrates, including hard surfaces, offering durability and resistance to fading. Both types cater to different printing needs and applications in textiles and promotional products.</p></p>
