<p><p>The automotive electric side window sunshade market is segmented into two main types: telescopic left and right, and telescopic up and down. The telescopic left and right sunshades extend horizontally, allowing customization for various window sizes and providing shade for side passenger windows. Conversely, the telescopic up and down sunshades operate vertically, retracting from the top of the door frame to block sunlight and reduce heat inside the vehicle. Both types enhance passenger comfort and contribute to energy efficiency by minimizing reliance on air conditioning.</p></p>
