<p><p>Radio altimeters are devices used to measure the altitude of an aircraft relative to the terrain directly beneath it. They operate by emitting radio waves towards the ground and calculating the time taken for the signal to bounce back. This technology plays a critical role in enhancing flight safety, particularly during landing and takeoff phases in low-visibility conditions. The growing demand for automation and advanced navigation systems in aviation has spurred the development and adoption of radio altimeters.</p><p>The Radio Altimeters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
