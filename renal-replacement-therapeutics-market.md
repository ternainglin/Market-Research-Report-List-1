<p><p>Renal Replacement Therapeutics (RRT) encompasses various medical interventions aimed at replacing the normal function of the kidneys in patients with renal failure. As kidney-related diseases continue to rise globally, the demand for RRT is steadily increasing, driving market growth.</p><p>The Renal Replacement Therapeutics Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period. Additionally, improved awareness and early diagnosis of renal diseases contribute to the growing demand for RRT options.</p><p>Latest trends in the market indicate a shift towards home-based dialysis solutions, as patients prefer the convenience and flexibility they offer. Overall, the market presents significant opportunities for stakeholders as they adapt to these evolving trends.</p></p>
