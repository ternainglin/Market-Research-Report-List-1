These systems utilize advanced techniques like frequency hopping and directional antennas to maintain signal integrity.</p><p>Anti-Spoof technologies, on the other hand, defend against deceptive signals that mimic legitimate GNSS signals, preventing unauthorized access and maintaining the authenticity of the location data. Both are crucial for secure military operations.</p></p>
