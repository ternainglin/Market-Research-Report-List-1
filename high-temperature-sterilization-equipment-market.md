Steam sterilization utilizes saturated steam to eliminate microorganisms, making it effective for heat-resistant materials. Dry sterilization employs hot air for sterilization, suitable for moisture-sensitive items. Other methods may include chemical sterilization or radiation techniques, catering to specialized needs in various industries like healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and laboratories. The choice of method depends on the nature of the materials and contamination risks.</p></p>
