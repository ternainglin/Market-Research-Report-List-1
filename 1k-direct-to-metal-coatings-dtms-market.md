<p><p>1K Direct-to-Metal Coatings (DTMs) are designed for easy application directly onto metal surfaces without the need for priming. The market is divided into waterborne and solventborne types. Waterborne DTMs are environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. In contrast, solventborne DTMs use organic solvents, offering faster drying times and superior adhesion but with higher VOC levels. Both types cater to various industrial applications, balancing performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
