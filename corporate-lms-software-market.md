<p><p>Corporate Learning Management System (LMS) software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based LMS solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing easy access from anywhere with internet connectivity. They offer scalability and reduced maintenance costs. On-premises LMS, however, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and security but requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different organizational needs and preferences in managing employee training and development.</p></p>
