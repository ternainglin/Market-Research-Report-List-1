<p><p>The Sleep Non-invasive Ventilator (NIV) market includes devices designed to assist patients with respiratory challenges during sleep. Key types are CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) and BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) ventilators. CPAP delivers a consistent air pressure to keep airways open, primarily treating obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, BiPAP provides two levels of pressure: a higher one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation, accommodating patients with varying respiratory conditions and enhancing comfort during use.</p></p>
