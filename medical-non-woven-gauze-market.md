<p><p>The medical non-woven gauze market is divided into sterile and non-sterile categories. Sterile gauze is designed for use in surgical environments or on open wounds, ensuring no pathogens are present to reduce infection risk. Non-sterile gauze, while still effective for absorbing fluids and providing cushioning, is primarily used for basic first aid or wound protection when sterility is not a critical concern.
