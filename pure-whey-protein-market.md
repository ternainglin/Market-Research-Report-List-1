<p><p>The Pure Whey Protein market consists of three main types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), and Whey Protein Hydrolysate (WPH). WPC contains 70-80% protein, retaining beneficial nutrients and fats, making it cost-effective. WPI is highly refined, containing over 90% protein with minimal lactose and fats, ideal for those focused on purity and muscle building. WPH undergoes partial hydrolysis for quicker absorption, making it suitable for athletes needing rapid recovery. Each type caters to different dietary needs and fitness goals.</p></p>
