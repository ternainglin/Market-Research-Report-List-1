<p><p>Deaerator tanks are essential in removing dissolved gases, particularly oxygen, from water used in industrial processes. The tray-type deaerator employs a series of trays to enhance gas removal as water flows over them, promoting effective degassing. In contrast, spray-type deaerators utilize a spray mechanism to disperse water, maximizing surface area and facilitating gas escape. Additionally, other types may include vacuum deaerators or hybrid models, each tailored to specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal operational efficiency and equipment longevity.</p></p>
