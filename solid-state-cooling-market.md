<p><p>The solid-state cooling market comprises three main types: single-stage, multi-stage, and thermocycler systems. Single-stage systems utilize a single thermoelectric module for efficient cooling in compact applications. Multi-stage systems stack multiple modules to achieve lower temperatures, suitable for more demanding thermal management requirements. Thermocyclers, often used in laboratories, utilize rapid temperature cycling for various applications, like PCR testing. Each type caters to specific cooling needs, ranging from consumer electronics to advanced scientific research.</p></p>
