<p><p>The hydrographic survey boats market is primarily divided into two types: monohull and multihull boats. Monohull hydrographic survey boats feature a single hull, offering stability and easier maneuverability in various water conditions, making them suitable for shallow waters. In contrast, multihull hydrographic survey boats have multiple hulls, providing enhanced stability, reduced wake, and increased deck space, making them ideal for larger, open waters and extensive surveys. Both types serve critical roles in marine mapping and data collection.</p></p>
