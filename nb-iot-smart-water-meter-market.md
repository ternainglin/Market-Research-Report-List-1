<p><strong>The NB-IoT Smart Water Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Wet Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NB-IoT smart water meter market can be categorized into two main types: dry type and wet type. Dry type meters are typically designed with a sealed design that prevents water ingress, ensuring accurate readings in low-flow conditions. In contrast, wet type meters are directly exposed to water flow and are suited for high-flow applications. Both types utilize NB-IoT technology for efficient data transmission, enabling remote monitoring, real-time analytics, and improved water management in urban and rural settings.</p></p>
