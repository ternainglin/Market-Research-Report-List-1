<p><strong>The Connector Protection Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connector Protection Devices (CPDs) are essential for ensuring the longevity and reliability of electrical connections. The market is divided into Type I and Type II categories. Type I devices are designed for primary protection, safeguarding connections against overcurrent and short circuits. Type II devices, on the other hand, offer secondary protection, addressing issues like voltage spikes and transients. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and durability of electrical systems, catering to different protective needs within various applications.</p></p>
