<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone construction sealants are used for bonding, sealing, and waterproofing in building applications. The market is divided into one-component and two-component types. One-component sealants are pre-mixed, ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient for general-purpose applications. Two-component sealants consist of a base and a curing agent, requiring mixing before use, offering superior performance for demanding applications like structural glazing or high-stress environments. Both types provide durability, flexibility, and weather resistance, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
