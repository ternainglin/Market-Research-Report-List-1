<p><strong>The Wastewater Aeration Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc Aeration Systems</li><li>Pipe Aeration Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wastewater aeration systems are essential for promoting biological treatment by increasing oxygen levels in wastewater. Disc aeration systems utilize rotating disks submerged in wastewater, facilitating oxygen transfer through a surface area, enhancing contact between air and water. Conversely, pipe aeration systems employ diffused air through porous pipes submerged in treatment basins, distributing fine bubbles that maximize oxygen transfer efficiency. Both systems are crucial for effective wastewater treatment, improving microbial activity and overall performance in treatment facilities.</p></p>
