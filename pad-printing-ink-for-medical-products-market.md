<p><p>Pad printing ink for medical products is essential for ensuring high-quality and durable prints on various medical devices. The market is divided into solvent-based and UV curing inks. Solvent-based inks offer excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for a variety of surfaces but may involve longer drying times. Conversely, UV curing inks provide rapid curing under UV light, delivering immediate handling and enhanced durability, ideal for intricate designs on medical components where quick production is vital.</p></p>
