<p><p>The kidney stone retrieval device market encompasses various types aimed at treating urinary stones. Lithotripters use focused shock waves to break stones into smaller pieces for easier passage. Stone removal devices, such as baskets or snares, physically extract stones from the urinary tract. Ureteral stents are flexible tubes placed in the ureter to ensure urine flow while obstructing stones, facilitating their eventual passage or removal. Together, these devices address the challenges posed by kidney stones in clinical settings.</p></p>
