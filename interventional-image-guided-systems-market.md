Interventional MRI Image-Guided Systems provide detailed soft tissue visualization, aiding complex surgeries. Interventional Ultrasound Image-Guided Systems use ultrasound for guidance, allowing for immediate feedback. The "Other" category includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems that combine multiple imaging modalities for advanced intervention capabilities.</p></p>
