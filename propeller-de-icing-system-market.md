<p><p>The propeller de-icing system market features several key types: </p><p>1. **Mechanical De-icing System**: Utilizes mechanical components to remove ice buildup through movement or vibration. </p><p>2. **Electric Pulse Anti-icing System**: Employs brief electric pulses to disrupt ice formation on propellers, preventing accumulation. </p><p>3. **Electric Heating Anti-icing System**: Harnesses electric resistance heating elements to provide consistent warmth, effectively melting ice and preventing its formation. </p><p>These systems enhance aircraft safety and operational efficiency in icy conditions, catering to different performance needs.</p></p>
