<p><strong>The Pneumatic Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way</li><li>Three-Way</li><li>Four-Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic valves market includes three primary types: Two-Way, Three-Way, and Four-Way valves. Two-Way valves control the flow of air in one direction, ideal for simple on-off applications. Three-Way valves manage airflow to alternate between two outputs, supporting more complex systems like actuators. Four-Way valves direct air to multiple outputs, commonly used in pneumatic cylinders to facilitate extension and retraction. Each type serves distinct functions, catering to various industrial needs and enhancing automation efficiency.</p></p>
