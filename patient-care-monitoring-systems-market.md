<p><p>Patient Care Monitoring Systems can be categorized into two main market types: multiple function and single function systems. Multiple function systems are integrated devices that simultaneously monitor various vital parameters such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation, providing comprehensive data for healthcare professionals. In contrast, single function systems focus on monitoring a specific parameter, like ECG or respiratory rates. While multiple function systems prioritize broad patient oversight, single function systems offer high precision and specialization for targeted health monitoring.</p></p>
