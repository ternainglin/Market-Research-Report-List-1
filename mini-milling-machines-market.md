<p><p>Mini milling machines are categorized into three main types based on their axis capabilities. 3-axis machines move along the X, Y, and Z axes, allowing for basic milling tasks and simple shapes. 4-axis machines add a rotational axis, enabling more complex designs and the ability to work on multiple sides of a material. 5-axis machines provide advanced capabilities, allowing for intricate shapes, precise angles, and complex geometries, making them ideal for high-precision applications in various industries.</p></p>
