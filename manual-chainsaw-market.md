<p><p>The manual chainsaw market is primarily divided into two types: single side saws and double sided saws. Single side saws feature cutting teeth on one side, making them ideal for precision work and maintaining control during cuts. In contrast, double sided saws have teeth on both sides, allowing for more versatile usage and efficiency in cutting tasks. Both types cater to different user needs, with single sided saws often preferred for detailed work and double sided saws for faster material removal.</p></p>
