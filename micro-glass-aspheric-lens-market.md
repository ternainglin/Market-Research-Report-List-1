<p><p>The micro glass aspheric lens market includes various types, each with unique features. Flat lenses have a flat surface, ideal for specific applications without distortion. Biconvex lenses are curved on both sides, commonly used for magnification. Double concave lenses, curved inward on both sides, are used to diverge light. Concave lenses curve inward, while convex lenses curve outward, both used in different optical devices. Each type serves specific needs in industries like healthcare, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
