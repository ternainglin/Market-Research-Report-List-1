<p><p>The sports sands market categorizes sand into four main types based on particle size. Fine sand has smaller grains, providing excellent drainage and a smooth playing surface, ideal for activities like volleyball. Medium fine sand balances texture and drainage, suitable for golf courses and athletic tracks. Medium sand offers a good compromise between stability and softness, often used in multi-sport facilities. Coarse sand, with larger particles, enhances durability and is commonly used in construction and base layers for sports fields.</p></p>
