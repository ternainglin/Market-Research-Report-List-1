<p><p>The soft fluoropolymer tubing market includes primarily PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) and ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) hoses, both known for their exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability. PTFE hoses are widely used in applications requiring high temperature and aggressive chemicals, offering low friction and excellent non-stick properties. ETFE hoses, while also resistant to chemicals, have enhanced mechanical strength and are more flexible, making them suitable for complex configurations and higher pressure situations. Together, they cater to diverse industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing.</p></p>
