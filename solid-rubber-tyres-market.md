<p><p>The solid rubber tyre market comprises two main types: cylindrical solid tires and oblique bottom solid tires. Cylindrical solid tires feature a uniform shape that provides enhanced stability and load-bearing capacity, making them ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, oblique bottom solid tires are designed with a slanted profile, which improves traction and maneuverability on uneven surfaces. Both types offer durability and reduced maintenance needs, appealing to sectors like warehousing, construction, and material handling.</p></p>
