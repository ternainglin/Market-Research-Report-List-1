<p><p>The Weld Scanner market encompasses various technologies used for weld inspection and quality assurance. Laser-based scanners utilize focused light to capture detailed profiles and detect surface imperfections in welded joints. Ultrasound scanners, on the other hand, employ high-frequency sound waves to penetrate materials, identifying internal defects and ensuring structural integrity. Other market types may include electromagnetic, eddy current, and visual inspection methods, each offering unique advantages for specific applications in evaluating weld quality and ensuring compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
