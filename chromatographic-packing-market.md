<p><p>The chromatographic packing market is categorized into two main types: inorganic and organic polymer packing. Inorganic packings, such as silica and alumina, provide high thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for varying separation techniques. In contrast, organic polymer packings, including polystyrene and polyethylene, offer flexibility and versatility in applications, particularly in biochromatography. Both types are essential for efficient separation processes in analytical chemistry, pharmaceuticals, and environmental testing, catering to diverse research and industrial needs.</p></p>
